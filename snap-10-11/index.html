Part 1:

Visit the page at https://mailchimp.com/help/upload-add-and-edit-images-in-campaigns/, pick one piece of functionality that they describe on that page, and write a user story for it. Remember, all user stories are of the form "As a _____ user, I can ______"

Part 2:
